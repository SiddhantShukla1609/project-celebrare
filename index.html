FAQ's by our users

How to create a wedding invitation?

It's a super easy process to create a wedding invitation with Celebrare:
1. Select the template
2. Click on "Try this card"
3. Fill the form with your details
4. Click on the "Share" button
5. Make the payment and download the card

--->

Can I add more than 4 events in my invitation?

Yes, it's possible. After filling the form, you can edit each page. Simply select the page and click on "Edit this page" to add a new text with your event name and details.

I am not able to write full names due to the word limit. Is there any solution?

Yes, you can fill the first name in the form, and after that, you will get an option to edit the page where you can add the full name.

How much time will it take to download the card after payment?

You will get your card instantly once you make the payment.

Can I add my own photos to the invitations?

Yes, you can add your photos to the card. After filling the details, you will get an option to edit the card where you can add your photos using the "Add Image" button.

Can I start my video with religious symbols like Ganesh ji, Bismillah, Mahaveer, Jesus, Buddha, or Gurunanak ji?

Yes, you can add your symbols or icons to the card. After filling the details, you will get an option to edit the card where you can add your symbols or icons using the "Add Icon" button.

After payment, will the watermark be removed?

Yes, after the payment, the watermark will be removed.

I have purchased the card but it is still showing me to purchase it?

In this case, you can directly contact us through WhatsApp or call.

Can I edit my card after purchase?

Yes, you can edit your card after purchase through "My Orders" section for free.

Can I add more pages to my card?

Yes, you can add more pages as per your need without any extra charge.

Can I add more text to my card after filling the form?

Yes, you can add new text to the card. After filling the details, you will get an option to edit the card where you can add new text using the "Add text" button.

Where will I get the option to purchase the card?

After you create your card, you will get an option to share at the top right corner. Click on it, and then you will get the option to purchase it or share it as a sample.
